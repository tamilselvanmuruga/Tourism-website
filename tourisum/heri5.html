GULMARG

Gulmarg, located in Jammu and Kashmir, is a stunning hill station known for its snow-capped mountains, lush meadows, and world-class skiing. It
attracts tourists year-round with activities like gondola rides, trekking, and winter sports.

Place To Be Visit

Gulmarg Gondola – High-altitude cable car with sweeping mountain views.

Apharwat Peak – Snowy summit ideal for skiing and adventure.

Biosphere Reserve – Wildlife sanctuary with rare Himalayan species.
