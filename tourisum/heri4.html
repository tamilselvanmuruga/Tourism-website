GANKTOK

Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Himalayas, known for its scenic landscapes, Buddhist monasteries, and vibrant
local culture. It is a popular tourist destination offering trekking, cable car rides, and views of Mt. Kanchenjunga.

Place To Be Visit

Tsomgo Lake – Sacred glacial lake with stunning mountain views.

Rumtek Monastery – Majestic Buddhist monastery with rich heritage.

Tashi Viewpoint – Scenic spot for Kanchenjunga sunrise views.
