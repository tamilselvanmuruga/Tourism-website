COORG

Coorg, also known as Kodagu, is a scenic hill station in Karnataka renowned for its coffee plantations, lush greenery, and misty landscapes.
It offers rich culture, adventure activities, and serene natural beauty, making it a popular tourist destination.

Place To Be Visit

Abbey Falls – A picturesque waterfall nestled in coffee plantations.

Raja’s Seat – A scenic viewpoint offering panoramic valley views.

Dubare Elephant Camp – Interact with and learn about elephants by the river.
