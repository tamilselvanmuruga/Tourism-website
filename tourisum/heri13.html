OOTY

Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a popular hill station in Tamil Nadu famed for its cool climate, tea gardens, and the scenic Nilgiri Hills
It attracts tourists with its botanical gardens, Ooty Lake, and the historic toy train ride.

Place To Be Visit

Ooty Lake – Serene artificial lake ideal for boating and lakeside strolls.

Doddabetta Peak – Highest Nilgiri summit with panoramic valley views.

Botanical Gardens – Lush garden with exotic plants and a fossil tree.
