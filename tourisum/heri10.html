MCLEODGANJ

McLeod Ganj, located in Himachal Pradesh, is a peaceful hill town known as the home of the Dalai Lama and a center of Tibetan culture. Surrounded
by pine forests and mountains, it's popular for monasteries, trekking trails, and spiritual retreats.

Place To Be Visit

Triund Hill – Scenic trek with panoramic views of the Dhauladhar range.

Bhagsu Waterfall – Cascading falls near a temple, perfect for a short hike.

Namgyal Monastery – Dalai Lama’s monastery and a hub of Tibetan spirituality.
