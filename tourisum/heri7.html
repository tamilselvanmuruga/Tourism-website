KODAIKANAL

Kodaikanal, located in Tamil Nadu, is a serene hill station known for its mist-covered cliffs, beautiful lakes, and lush green valleys. Often called
the "Princess of Hill Stations," it offers a peaceful retreat with boating, trekking, and scenic viewpoints.

Place To Be Visit

Kodaikanal Lake – Star-shaped lake perfect for boating and lakeside strolls.

Coaker’s Walk – Scenic cliffside path with panoramic valley views.

Pillar Rocks – Three towering granite pillars surrounded by misty forests.
